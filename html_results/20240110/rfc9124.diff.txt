If the manifest is stored in NVRAM prior to processing, the integrated payload may cause the manifest to exceed the available storage. Because the manifest is received prior to validation of applicability, authority, or correctness, integrated payloads cause the recipient to expend network bandwidth and energy that may not be required if the manifest is discarded, and these costs vary with the size of the integrated payload.<a href="#section-4.5.12-3" class="pilcrow">¶</a></p>
